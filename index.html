Our Story

"When we first met, we were strangers in a world of differences, never imagining we'd become the ones who fit. Yet here we are, proof that the most unlikely bonds Can turn into the love we never knew we’d find."

  • First Met

    Somewhere around late 2020

    We first met as colleagues at Prudential, working in the same department but on different teams. Our teams were based in different branches, so our interactions were limited. We barely knew each other beyond our names, faces, and titles.

  • First Interaction

    Jun 2022

    Our first real interaction was during a two-day training held by the company, where we were placed in the same team. We shared laughs and quickly felt comfortable with each other. However, it wasn't the right time, as one of us was not single. We left with good memories and moved forward with our lives.

  • First date

    Somewhere around late April 23

    Our paths crossed again during a trip to Indonesia with friends. The seven-day journey gave us plenty of time to get to know each other, and that's when we started developing feelings. After returning to Vietnam, we continued hanging out as friends, and our bond quickly grew into something romantic. We still joke about our first date, as we're not entirely sure which one should be considered the official start.

  • Proposal

    15th Sep 2024

    We often joked about marriage without truly expecting an answer. But over time, those jokes turned into something real as we began to imagine a future together. We grew accustomed to each other's presence, finding comfort and security in what we shared. Then, on September 15th, Hoang officially proposed with a ring passed down from his mom—a piece of family heritage. And now, here we are, building on that moment with the wedding you see today.

Hai Phong's intro

Hai Phong’s culinary scene is dominated by fresh seafood, particularly dishes like bánh đa cua, bún cá. The city also offers scenic attractions, including the beautiful Do Son Beach and Cat Ba Island, perfect for those seeking both culture and nature.

Hanoi's intro

Hanoi’s charming streets are home to iconic landmarks such as the Hoàn Kiếm Lake and the Temple of Literature. The city is famous for its unique food culture, with dishes like phở, bún chả, and egg coffee that showcase the local flavors and traditions of northern Vietnam.
